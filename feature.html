Core Features

Built Around Your Trust

Three pillars that make every order from Fresh & Organic worth it.

Advanced Cold Storage
Our facility maintains a precise 2°C environment that preserves freshness, nutrients and shelf life of every product — from leafy greens to tropical fruits.
  • Maintained at 2°C round the clock
  • Humidity-controlled chambers
  • Separate zones for fruits & vegetables
  • Power backup — no temperature breaks
100% Organic Certified
Every product in our catalogue is sourced directly from certified organic farms — no pesticides, no artificial ripening, no compromise on purity.
  • Direct farm partnerships
  • No pesticides or chemicals
  • No artificial ripening agents
  • Freshness score shown on every product
Fast & Safe Delivery
Orders are packed in insulated cold-chain boxes and dispatched the same day — ensuring your produce arrives as fresh as it left our facility.
  • Same-day dispatch
  • Insulated cold-chain packaging
  • Live order status tracking
  • Free delivery on online payment
How It Works

From Farm to Your Door

A simple, transparent process — so you always know where your food comes from and how it gets to you.

Start Shopping
1
Farm Harvest
Organic produce is harvested fresh from partner farms — hand-picked at peak ripeness, sorted and quality-checked on-site.
2
Cold Storage Intake
Within hours of harvest, produce enters our 2°C facility. Graded by size, variety and quality — stored in humidity-controlled chambers.
3
You Place an Order
Browse our organic range, add to cart, apply a coupon, choose payment — UPI, card or COD — and confirm your order in under 2 minutes.
4
Packed & Dispatched
Your order is packed in insulated cold-chain boxes same day. Track it live — from Order Placed → Confirmed → Packed → Out for Delivery → Delivered.
5
Fresh at Your Door
Your produce arrives fresh, cold and intact — exactly as it left our facility.

Everything You Get

Every detail — from the app to the packaging — is designed with your experience in mind.

Secure Authentication
Login and register with Firebase Auth, including Google Sign-In. Your account, cart, orders and profile are fully protected — login required to access.
Live Order Tracking
Flipkart-style order status tracker. Watch your order move from Order Placed → Confirmed → Packed → Out for Delivery → Delivered in real time.
Coupons & Discounts
Apply coupon codes at checkout for instant savings. FRESH10, ORGANIC20, SAVE50 — real discount logic, not fake numbers.
Fully Responsive
Designed mobile-first. Every page works perfectly on phones, tablets and desktops using Bootstrap 5 responsive grid.
Multiple Payment Modes
Pay via UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), Credit/Debit Card (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), Pay in 3 instalments, or Cash on Delivery.

Every product shows a live freshness percentage and rating — so you know exactly what you're getting before you order.
